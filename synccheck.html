Sync Comms Check

Runs a live end-to-end test against your deployed sync backend — reachability, merge, two-writer race, delete durability, and latency — from this device, over the same network the camp will use. It adds a few clearly-marked test records and removes them again; your real board data is not touched.
Tip: run this once from a TOC laptop and once from a phone on the field network. Keep the link private.
